Financial Management and Fundraising for Women Entrepreneurs in WASH ▼
WASH Innovation Hub (WIH) - Urban Management - ASCI, in collaboration with Water.org, conducts workshops on enhancing skills in financial management and fundraising for women entrepreneurs in WASH. The program covers a range of vital topics, including various funding mechanisms, Intellectual Property Rights, strategies for fundraising, the Investment Readiness Rating Tool, and detailed sessions on due diligence. It servs as a dynamic networking platform, linking participants with seasoned investors and industry stalwarts, thereby cultivating significant connections for those at the forefront of the WASH sector.
